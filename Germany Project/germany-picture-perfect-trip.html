Picture Perfect Trip: Germany

Where would you go?

I would go to Germany! I've always wanted to explore the historic cities, see the beautiful countryside, and try the delicious food.

What would you do?

  1. Visit Berlin and see the famous landmarks like the Brandenburg Gate and Checkpoint Charlie.
  2. Explore the historic city of Munich and visit the Marienplatz and the Neuschwanstein Castle.
  3. Take a scenic drive through the Black Forest region and stop at picturesque towns along the way.
  4. Visit the stunning Rhine Valley and take a river cruise to see the castle-lined hillsides.

What would you eat?

  • Currywurst - a popular street food made with grilled sausage and curry ketchup.
  • Schnitzel - a thin, breaded and fried cutlet of meat, typically pork.
  • Bretzel - a traditional German pretzel that's soft, chewy, and delicious.
  • Black Forest Cake - a rich and decadent cake made with layers of chocolate cake, whipped cream, and cherries.
